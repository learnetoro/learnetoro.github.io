This video talks about how you can take your profits when you copytrade on Etoro, and what happens to your copytrading profits if you just leave them in the site... Do you have to withdraw your profits and re-add them to the copy? Or does it take care of that automatically? Also, if you want to take your profits out of a copy-trade, does that mean you have to stop copying that trader or can you just take some of the money out and leave the rest to still copy that person? Etoro have done a pretty fantastic job of designing their system to optimise the performance of your money. Whatever profits you make from copying a trader are automatically reinvested back into that copy so the amount of funds you're copying that trader with can just keep on growing, making bigger and bigger trade sizes (provided the trader you're copying is making profits rather than losses...) If you really want to withdraw money from a copy position, then you can either close the copy outright in which case your money (after profits or losses are applied) will go back into your available balance, and you can then withdraw it from Etoro, OR you can choose just to remove a portion of those funds from the copy, but still keep some of your money with that trader, still copying them.
