Main Copy Trading Page: https://etoro.tw/2fMizH2 . When it's time to remove funds from your copy trading position on Etoro, there's a few ways to do it. The Stop Copy button will automatically close all of your trades and return your balance to your "Available funds". Using the Pause Copy feature or manually closing the trades will free up money within the copy trade and then you can use the "Remove Funds" button to move money back to your available balance. From there you can use it to trade any asset, copy anyone else you want to, or withdraw however much you want from Etoro back to your bank account (or wherever you credited your account from) Here's a my beginners' playlists if you'd like to find out more: Beginners' Tutorials: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qeBPdi5LR8&list=PLSp_9hRpu6lKOj-KjMnlULlG62zXNIBz9 Copying Tutorials: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5FHxBW2u4oE&list=PLSp_9hRpu6lINmN095kN6-bLYAQi7SiJa
