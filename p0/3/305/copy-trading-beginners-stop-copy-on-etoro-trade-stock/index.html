How to Stop Copying A trader on Etoro. Here's a link to the Main Copy Trading Page: https://etoro.tw/2fMizH2 As part of protecting your assets, sometimes you need to stop copying someone you've previously invested in. It's a very simple process, done from your portfolio screen. When you actually click the stop button, the system will instantly try to close all of the opened positions which that trader has made for you - the relevant markets have to be open for this to happen, and if not, then Etoro will wait until they are and then close all the open positions. Once this is done, all money (+ profit or - loss) will be returned to your 'available' funds from where you can withdraw them or put them to work on any other asset.
