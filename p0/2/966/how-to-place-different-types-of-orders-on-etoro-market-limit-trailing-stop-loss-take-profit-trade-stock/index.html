Description

In this video we explain the different types of orders on eToro US. 0:23 - The Amounts/Units box - what this means and how to use it 1:33 - Buying (Trade) - comparable to a “market” order 1:55 - Buying (order) - comparable to a “limit” order 2:40 - How to view your pending orders. You can also go to this link: https://www.etoro.com/portfolio/orders 2:59 - Selling (Market) - we also cover how to sell all of a particular asset at once 3:24 - Selling (Part of a Trade) - allows you to sell part of an asset 4:08 - Selling (UI explanation) - we break down the different elements that are shown on the “Edit Trade” screen 4:48 - Selling (Stop Loss) - allows you to set a rate (or price) at which your position will close (sell) 5:46 - Selling (Trailing Stop Loss) - allows your stop loss rate to track upwards with the price of the asset 6:54 - Selling (Take Profit) - The take profit option allows you to set a rate (or price) above the current price. If this rate is reached, the position will close automatically. Note: “take profit” does not mean just “taking profits,” this will close the entire associated position. entity name: eToro USA LLC etorous
